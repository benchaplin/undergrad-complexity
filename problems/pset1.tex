{\bf Problem 1}
A variadic function $f: \N^* \rightarrow \N$ is called a {\bf coding function} if there are "inverse" functions
$g: \N \rightarrow \N$ and $h: \N \times \N \rightarrow \N$ such that:

\begin{align*}
	g(f(a_1, \ldots, a_n))    & = n                    \\
	h(f(a_1, \ldots, a_n), i) & = a_i, i \leq i \leq n
\end{align*}
